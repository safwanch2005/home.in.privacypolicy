Privacy Policy

Welcome to Home.in, a mobile application developed using Flutter with Firebase as the backend (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our Service. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile picture
  • Authentication details (Google, phone number, email-password)
  • Location data (only if you allow location permission)

Property Information

Users can post properties which include details such as:

  • Property type (house, apartment, land, etc.)
  • Sale or rent status
  • Ownership details (owner or dealer)
  • Property description
  • Images (minimum of 4 per property)

Usage Data

We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used, including but not limited to:

  • Chat messages and images exchanged between users
  • Notifications received and interaction with them
  • Properties added to favorites
  • Search queries and filters used

How We Use Your Information

Personal Information

To facilitate the creation of your user account and secure authentication. To allow you to post properties and manage your listings. To enable communication between users via chat. To send notifications regarding new messages, property approvals, and other updates.

Property Information

To display properties to other users after admin approval. To help users find properties by category, recent listings, and nearby properties using location data. To improve our search functionality and filtering options.

Usage Data

To monitor and analyze the usage of the Service for improvements. To provide and maintain the functionality of the Service. To detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect and defend our rights and property.
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
  • To protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.
  • To trusted third-party service providers, such as Firebase, that store data securely on our behalf in compliance with industry standards and certifications (e.g., ISO 27001, SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3).

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Encryption

All sensitive customer information, including physical addresses, email addresses, and any potential credit information, is encrypted both at rest and in transit. We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.2 or higher for data in transit.

Secure Authentication

Our application utilizes Firebase Authentication, which supports secure methods such as email/password, phone authentication, and third-party providers like Google and Facebook. This ensures that user authentication is handled securely.

Database Security

We use Firebase Firestore, a secure database service provided by Google Cloud. Firestore offers robust security features, including role-based access control (RBAC), to ensure that only authorized users can access specific data.

Third-Party Services

We leverage Firebase's secure data storage services, which comply with industry standards and certifications, including ISO 27001, SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3. This ensures that customer data is stored securely and handled in accordance with best practices.

Regular Security Audits

Our application undergoes regular security audits and assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities. This proactive approach helps us maintain a high level of security for our users.
